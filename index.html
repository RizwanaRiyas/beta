Automobile Engineering

Automobile engineering is your gateway to the exciting world of designing, building, and maintaining the vehicles that shape our lives. This dynamic field blends mechanical, electrical, software, and safety engineering to create everything from sleek cars and motorbikes to powerful trucks and specialized machines. Our comprehensive program equips you with the skills to navigate every stage of an automobile's lifecycle.

2 years

Electronics Engineering

Become an Electronics Engineer or PCB Designer, or take a hands-on approach as a Home Appliances Technician, DTH Installation & Service Technician, or Solar Panel Installation & Service Technician. This course equips you for a future where electronics and communication power every aspect of our lives.

2 years

Refrigeration & Airconditioning

As global temperatures climb, the demand for skilled refrigeration and air-conditioning technicians is booming. This course opens doors to a multitude of exciting career paths in this ever-growing field.This course equips you with the skills to design, develop, manufacture, simulate, and repair refrigeration and airconditioning systems. You will delve into the science behind heating, cooling, humidifying, dehumidifying, cleaning, ventilation, and system effectiveness.

2 years

Civil Engineering

You will learn to design safe and efficient structures and understand soil mechanics through structural and geotechnical engineering. You will also plan roads and manage water supply systems through transportation and water resources engineering.Graduates can work for construction or consulting firms, contributing to government infrastructure projects.

Computer Engineering

The Computer Engineering course equips you with the skills to design, construct, and oversee computer systems, encompassing both software and hardware.This course is your launchpad to a flourishing career, opening doors to a multitude of job opportunities in esteemed companies.

Who Are We?

Beta Institute of Engineering offers two-year engineering certificate courses under the Kerala SBTE, focusing on skill-based, job-oriented training in five branches. The institute combines modern lab facilities, project-based learning, and industry collaboration to prepare students for domestic and international job markets. Students also benefit from personality development programs, extracurricular activities, and a strong placement support system.
